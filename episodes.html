Episode 1: The City on the Great Pit

Riko is a young apprentice cave raider living in Orth, the city built on the edge of the Abyss. One day during cave raiding, Riko's friend, Nat, was attacked by the giant serpent beast, Crimson Splitjaw. After taking the beast's attention from Nat, Riko is then chased by the serpent. At the most dangerous moment, the area is suddenly enveloped in light, accompanied by a thundering sound.
