The Abyss

The Abyss is a gigantic unexplored chasm stretching deep into the earth. It is filled with ancient relics, dangerous creatures, and mysteries that have attracted Cave Raiders from around the world for generations.

As explorers descend deeper, the challenges become greater and the Curse of the Abyss makes returning to the surface increasingly difficult.
